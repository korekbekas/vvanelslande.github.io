##### What's vvctre?

A Nintendo 3DS emulator for Windows and Linux.

##### How do I install plugins?

Put the .dll or .so in vvctre's folder.

##### Will you add Android support?

No.

##### Will you add iOS support?

No.

##### Will you add macOS support?

No.
